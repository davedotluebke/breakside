Breakside — Field Position UI v2

Spatial event entry. Portrait = embedded in the real app's Field tab (full chrome). Landscape = full-screen takeover with a minimal header (the coach is on a sideline). All three placement gestures are live at once. Guide lines: white dashes split home/middle/away lanes; red dashes mark the O & D red zones (20 yd). Tap a player then a spot, tap an empty spot to pick from a popover, or drag a player onto the field. Drag any of the last 4 markers to fine-tune; older plays fade.

Tip: the ⛶ button in the header toggles portrait ⇄ landscape full-screen, the same affordance a coach would use mid-game.
