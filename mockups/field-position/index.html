Breakside — Field Position UI

Interactive mockup for spatial event entry (Full mode). Switch orientation, offense/defense, and the throw-entry interaction model to compare. The field shows realistic 3:1 proportions, drawn endzones, and dotted guide lines that split the playing field into 9 sections (3 lanes × 3 depths incl. red zones). Tap to actually record throws — the disc advances, arrows draw, the log fills.
