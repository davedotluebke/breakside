⚡

Pick Your Detail

Track only scores and playing time, or full throw-by-throw possessions, or use Field Mode to spatially place every event on the field—switch modes mid-game without losing a beat.

🥏

Field Mode

Tap a drawn field to place every throw, catch, drop, and D exactly where it happened. Rotate to landscape for a full-screen field.

🎙️

AI Speech Narration Beta

Describe plays out loud and Breakside turns your words into structured events. Opt-in per session.

📊

Real-Time Stats

Goals, assists, completion %, breaks, holds, Ds, and exact playing time—per game, per tournament, or across the whole season.

🔀

Smart Line Management

Set the next line—and the one after—with O/D lines and On Deck. Live playing-time and gender-ratio cues help you balance points.

🔄

Multi-Coach Sync

One coach can do it all, or coaches can collaborate in real time—one tracks plays, another sets lines—with role handoffs and a Lineup Ready ping.

🏆

Tournaments & Events

Group games into an event with a shared roster, tag them by phase (Day 1, Pool, Bracket…), and view aggregate stats.

📺

Live Viewer

Share a link so parents and fans can follow the score and play-by-play in real time.

📱

Offline-First PWA

Full functionality on the sideline with no signal. Installs like a native app and syncs automatically when connected.

📈

Export Your Data

Multi-sheet Excel (.xlsx) workbooks and JSON export for stats, event rosters, and team rosters—your data is yours.

Install in Seconds

Breakside is a Progressive Web App—install it like a native app, no app store required.

🍎

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open Safari and go to breakside.pro
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" to install
🤖

Android

  1. Open Chrome and go to breakside.pro
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap "Install" to add
